### What is this
This is my public knowledge base, where I share research, technical insights, and notes on my learning adventures.

> [!NOTE]
> These are my research notes as opposed to published work, while I have tried to make them neat and tidy, some of these are still works in progress and a bit messy.

### How it works
I write and edit all of my notes in markdown through Obsidian, as I am not able to make all of my notes public these are uploaded to a private GitHub repo. Every commit a GitHub action runs that scans my notes for publishable files and copies them over to my quartz directory for hosting. In order to publish just add `publish: true` to the front matter of the note.
